How and Why to Use the App

This section explains the value of the platform in a simple way for possible users.

For Eventgoers

Find events, keep track of your schedule, manage your saved or registered events, and build connections with other attendees.

For Businesses

View events on a calendar, manage event operations, and track KPIs that help measure performance and growth.

For Admins

Monitor platform activity, review event and user management tools, and use analytics to support decisions across the system.

Calendar View

Businesses use the calendar to track event dates, availability, and scheduling at a glance. Business can view their monthly event calendar, create new events, and alter existing ones.

Event Management

Businesses can create, update, and monitor the status of events. Users can create and update event descriptions to reflect changes. Events are moved to the approved list once verified by the admin. They also have access to archived events.

KPI Dashboard

Businesses are enabled to make smarter decisions with marketing strategies, event management, and standard business operations through ready to-use Key Performance Indicators. The platform automatically monitors valuable statistics like top performing events, popular event categories, and event sign-ups.

Calendar View

Our admin uses the calendar to oversee system-wide activity and review scheduled events across the platform over a month.

Event Management

Our admin has authoritative power to review, approve, edit, or manage events posted by businesses. The admin is responsible for maintaining quality across the platform, mitigating duplicate events, and preventing event listings that go against terms and conditions.

User Management

Similar to event mangagement, our admin monitor users, permissions, account issues, and platform access from this screen. Admin is repsonsible for maintianing a safe and friendly environment for all platform users.

KPI Dashboard

The dashboard automatically provides statistics for platform-wide performance and trends to support decisions, identify issues, and measure engagement. It provides benchmarks and highlights unique eventgoer and business activity.
